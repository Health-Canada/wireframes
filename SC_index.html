LETTER SENT TO ASSOCIATIONS

The Submission Certification Form that was issued on March 3, 1995 has been revised as attached. The electronic version of the revised form is available on the Drugs Directorate Bulletin Board.

As stated in the covering letter of the previously issued form, a completed Submission Certification Form is required with all original and solicited information and material related to New Drug Submissions, Supplemental New Drug Submissions and Notifiable Changes. This includes the original submission, submission updates, and responses to Screening Deficiency Notices, Notices of Deficiency, and Notices of Noncompliance.

Please advise your members that only those parts of the form that are applicable to the submission being filed are to be included. For example, a New Drug Submission that includes pre-clinical and clinical studies must include all parts of the form. A New Drug Submission that includes only comparative bioavailability studies must include part 1 of the form. Notifiable Changes should include only the part 1 of the form. If you or your member have any questions about the form or its use, please contact the Submission and Information Policy Division, Bureau of Drug Policy and Coordination, at (613)941-0827.
