Drug and Health Product Search

Search for consumer information on drug products, licenced natural health products, licenced medical devices, safety and common and reported side effects.

You may search using 3 ways:

1) brand name
2) active ingredient
3) identification number

Use the asterisk (*) to broaden or refine your search. This symbol will help you find everything related to your search term.

As the Drug and Health Product Register is a pilot project, it currently only provides the top 100 prescribed brand name and generic drug products. These drugs have been selected based on the top Canadian sales for 2013.

An additional 250 products are included that consist largely of generic drugs, but do include some brand name products. These have the same active ingredient(s) as the top 100.
