Regulatory Decision Summary Search

Regulatory Decision Summaries (RDS) explain Health Canada’s decision for certain health products (including prescription drugs and medical devices) seeking market authorisation. The RDSs include the purpose of the submission and the reason for the decision. Enter text below to search for an RDS by drug/device name, medicinal ingredient, manufacturer, or DIN/Licence Number.
