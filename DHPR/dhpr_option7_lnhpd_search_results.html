Products with a Natural Health Product Licence:

  • Have been assessed by Health Canada and found to be safe, effective and of high quality under their conditions of use
  • Are identified by an 8-digit Natural Product Number (NPN) or Homeopathic Medicine Number (DIN-HM)
  • Includes:
    • Vitamin and mineral supplements
    • Herb and plant-based remedies
    • Traditional medicines like Traditional Chinese Medicines (TCM) or Ayurvedic (Indian) medicines
    • Omega 3 and essential fatty acids
    • Probiotics
    • Homeopathic medicines
    • Many everyday consumer products, like certain toothpastes, antiperspirants, shampoos, facial products and mouthwashes

Information on natural health products you can find here includes:

  • Product name
  • Product licence holder
  • Natural Product Number (NPN) or Homeopathic Medicine Number (DIN-HM)
  • Product’s medicinal ingredients
  • Product’s non-medicinal ingredients
  • Product’s dosage form
  • Product’s recommended use or purpose (i.e. it’s health claim or indication)
  • Risk information associated with the product’s uses (i.e. cautions, warning, contra-indications and known adverse reactions)

Additional Information:

  • Data is updated nightly
  • Use the User Guide for help in advance search
  • Use the Terminology Guide to understand the words and terms used in the database
  • Licensed natural health product data can be extracted using the LNHPD Data Extract

For more information:

  • For technical support or for general questions about the content, please contact the Natural Health Products Directorate at nhp_initiative_psn@hc-sc.gc.ca
  • To search detailed descriptions of specific NHP Ingredients, see the Natural Health Products Ingredient Database
  • For more information about how natural health products are licensed, see About Natural Health Product Regulation in Canada
